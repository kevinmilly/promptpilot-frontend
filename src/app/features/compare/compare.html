📘 Understanding Your Metrics

⚡ Latency: How long the model took to respond (in milliseconds). Lower is faster.

💬 Tokens: The approximate number of tokens used. More tokens = longer or more complex responses.

💰 Cost: The estimated price for generating the response, based on tokens and each model’s rate.

🧠 Readability: Measures how easy the text is to read — higher scores mean simpler, clearer writing.

🎭 Tone: The overall emotional or stylistic tone detected (e.g., neutral, positive, formal).

🔗 Coherence: How logically connected and consistent the response is compared to other models.
